Privacy Policy

Effective date: 27 April 2026

Thinking Chimp PLT (“we”, “our”, or “us”) respects your privacy. This policy describes how we handle information in connection with the thinkingchimp.com website and our products, including the Trickl app.

This website

The thinkingchimp.com website does not use cookies, tracking scripts, or analytics services. We do not collect any personal information through this website.

If you contact us by email, we will use your email address only to respond to your enquiry. We do not share it with third parties.

Trickl app

Trickl is currently in development and not yet available. When it launches, this policy will be updated to describe precisely what data the app collects, how it is stored, and how it is used.

Trickl is designed so that your personal data — your habits, progress, and related information — remains private to you. We do not collect, store, or have access to this data. It lives on your own device and personal storage, not on our servers.

We do not sell user data or use it for advertising purposes.

Your rights

You have the right to access, correct, or delete any personal data we hold about you. To make such a request, please contact us by email.

Data storage and security

Any personal data shared with us (such as an email address sent via direct email) is stored securely and accessed only when necessary to respond to your enquiry.

Changes to this policy

We may update this policy from time to time, particularly as Trickl nears launch. We will update the effective date above when changes are made. We encourage you to review this page periodically.
