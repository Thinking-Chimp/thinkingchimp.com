About Thinking Chimp

An independent software studio in Kuala Lumpur, Malaysia.

Who we are

Thinking Chimp PLT is a boutique software studio based in Kuala Lumpur, Malaysia. We make a small number of things, and we make them well — spending as much time understanding a problem as we do solving it.

We are a small, deliberate team. The best software is specific, unhurried, and honest.

Our work

Our current focus is behaviour design — software that applies the science of habit formation to help people make meaningful, lasting changes in their lives.

Our first product, Trickl, is a habit design app for iPhone and Apple Watch, grounded in BJ Fogg’s Tiny Habits research. It helps people design habits with intention, diagnose why they aren’t sticking, and practise until they become second nature.

How we work

We work slowly and carefully. Before writing a line of code, we spend time with the problem and the people who have it. Nothing ships until we believe the approach is right.

We are independently funded — which means we answer only to the people who use our software.
